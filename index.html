Cyber Panda Studios

A gaming studio dedicated to creating a fun and engaging experience for all our players!

Cyber Panda Studios was created by six Louisiana State University students to create an initial project. Six computer science students came together to form the studio, all with a passion for creating innovative and engaging games. The group would form during a computer science 4263 class under Professor Andrew Webb. This website displays our team members, current projects, and how to contact our studio for more information. We will be posting our game on the Steam platform for PC users and will be working on implementations for consol and other platforms as well. Make sure to check out our current projects and our steam greenlight when we upload our project to steam. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns about our team members or project please see the contact form below.

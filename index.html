Cyber Panda Studios

A gaming studio dedicated to creating a fun and engaging experience for all players!

Cyber Panda Studios was created by six Louisiana State University students. These six computer science students came together with a passion for creating innovative and engaging games. The group formed during a computer science 4263 class under Professor Andrew Webb. This website displays our team members, current projects, and how to contact our studio for more information. We will be posting our game on the Steam platform for PC users and will be working on implementations for consol and other platforms as well. Make sure to check out our current projects and our steam greenlight when we upload our project to steam. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns see the contact form below.

Project Road Rage

Project Description

The driver starts in the spawn area with a basic car. After learning the game mechanics the driver is then allowed into the chaotic open world. The goal is exploration. In order to explore the driver must upgrade the garage, take back the gas stations, and unlock new unique cars. The different terrains will have special abilites for certain cars. So, keep an eye out while exploring and beware of terrain leaders.
